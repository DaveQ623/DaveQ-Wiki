## Overview

### General Information

IRONMAN: YES

FREQUENCY: HOURLY

MEMBERS: YES

Ironmen and regular Group Ironmen can participate. Competitive Group Ironmen only receive drops when their group completes the entire kill.

### Combat

COMBAT: YES

DEATH: NOT SAFE

Normal death and item-reclaim rules apply.

## Event Summary

Demon Flash Mobs are hourly combat encounters that appear in one of 14 possible locations. Each world has its own schedule. A demonic-energy announcement identifies the location 15 minutes before the demons spawn.

The mob contains one demon boss, two black demons, four lesser demons, and six imp heralds. The boss despawns 45 minutes after spawning if it has not been defeated.

## Getting There

Watch for the demonic-energy announcement in chat, then follow the demon symbol on the world map. A moving demon symbol means the boss is wandering and is probably not currently in combat.

Some encounters spawn in the Wilderness. Certain locations are multi-combat areas, and players who have opted into PvP should avoid bringing items they are unwilling to lose.

## Quick Strategy

### Recommendations

- Bring appropriate combat equipment and food.
- Keep Freedom available for certain damage-over-time effects.
- Read the boss's full name before engaging because it identifies its modifiers.
- Watch the map icon and chat announcements to locate the active event.

### Warnings

- Most prayers and Ancient Curses empower the boss's attacks. Protect Item is the exception.
- Protection and deflection prayers do not reduce the empowered damage.
- Blood spells heal the demon boss and should not be used.
- Freedom clears damage-over-time effects from Blazing, Infernal, and Rending bosses.
- No rewards are given if the boss despawns.

### Strategy

1. Defeat the imp heralds to interrupt the boss's health siphon.
2. Defeat the lesser demons.
3. Defeat the black demons.
4. Finish the demon boss.

The boss begins with half of its maximum health and restores health by siphoning surviving demons. The exact attack order can change as minions are defeated or completely drained.

The boss's prefix identifies a combat bonus:

- Castellan: Increased Defence
- Deacon: Increased Magic
- Executioner: High-damage attacks
- General: Increased Attack

Its suffix identifies a special mechanic, including burning, poison, stuns, bleeding, area damage, stat drains, or reduced adrenaline gain.

## Rewards

### Reward Eligibility

- Deal at least 500 damage to the boss in a single hit.
- Receive the powerful-blow confirmation message.
- Remain in combat until the boss dies.
- Qualify before the boss reaches 10% health.

Leaving the fight or dying removes eligibility until another qualifying hit is dealt. Players joining after the boss reaches 10% health are too late to qualify.

### Drop Table

- Demon slayer equipment
- Demonic title scrolls
- Demon claws
- Herbs, dragonhides, and other resources

Wilderness encounters have a better chance of awarding demon slayer equipment, and their standard drops are generally more valuable.

## Achievements

- Daemonology of Celerity: Claim the malleus daemoniorum.
- 61 Shades of Red: Obtain the required collection of unique Slayer drops from Daemonheim and the surrounding area.
